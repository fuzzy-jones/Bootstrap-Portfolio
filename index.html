About Me

Hello my name is Fuzzy Jones, while that isn't my real name, I've been called Fuzzy since the day I was born. I actually didn't know my real name until my first day of kindergarden, when my mom informed me that the teacher would call me 'Donald". I cried because I didn't want that name and so it's remained Fuzzy ever since.

I am originally from a small town in South Jersey called Cinnaminson. I moved to Charlotte, NC in 2006 where I attended UNCC and received a bachelors degree in Marketing. After working in advertising and screen printing for a few years, I decided I wanted to make a career of building things. So I went back to school to become a welder. I held many certifications and worked in various power plants and refineries for a couple years. Recently I decided to pursue welding only as a hobby, but continue a career in building things, and have found my way to Web Development.

I grew up playing a lot of golf and video games, but now a days it's way more video games. I have also been obsessed with horror movies since I was about 3 years old, and I have a growing collection of horror action figures and horror tattoos. I got married this year, and being that my wife is from Japan I have been soaking up as much of her culture as possible in order to fulfill my destiny of becoming a samurai. Not really, but I like to tell everyone that to embarress her.
